Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How can I book a flight?

A1: You can book a flight directly from our booking dashboard. Choose your departure and arrival cities, select a flight, and proceed to checkout.

Q2: What if I need to cancel my flight?

A2: You can cancel your flight by visiting the "My Tickets" section and following the cancellation process. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.
